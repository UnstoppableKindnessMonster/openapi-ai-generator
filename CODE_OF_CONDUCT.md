# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) v2.1.

## Our Standards

We are committed to a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful environment for everyone, regardless of background or identity. We expect all participants to engage constructively, assume good faith, and treat others with professionalism.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers at **conduct@example.com**. All reports will be reviewed and addressed promptly and confidentially.

For the full text of the Contributor Covenant v2.1, see:
https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/
